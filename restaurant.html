BEST RESTAURANTS

Osteria Francescana (Modena, Emilia-Romagna)

Chef Massimo Bottura's three-Michelin-starred restaurant consistently ranks among the world's best, offering innovative interpretations of traditional Italian dishes with a modern twist.

Le Calandre (Rubano, Veneto)

Led by Chef Massimiliano Alajmo, this three-Michelin-starred restaurant showcases creative and refined cuisine in a sophisticated setting, emphasizing the finest ingredients and culinary techniques.

Piazza Duomo (Alba, Piedmont)

Chef Enrico Crippa's three-Michelin-starred restaurant celebrates the flavors of Piedmont with seasonal, farm-to-table dishes inspired by nature and tradition.
